Impermanent Loss
- temporary loss of funds when providing liquidity
- observed in standard liquidity pools
- LP must provide correct assets in correct ratio
- if liquidity provider withdraws liquidity than loss becomes permanent 
- pools outside of uniswap can also result in impremanent loss
- curve pools, balancer pools
